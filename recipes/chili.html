Chili

Description

This chili is easy, cooks in a few hours. It doesn't need a lot of reparation.

Ingredients

  • Ground turkey, 1 pound
  • one packet chili seasoning
  • one can light red kidney beans
  • one 28+ oz can diced tomatoes
  • one green pepper
  • one small onion
  • One bag of cook-in-a-bag of rice (wheat or white)

Steps

  1. Brown the turkey
  2. While turkey is browning:
    • dice the green pepper and onion
    • open the cans
  3. Put the tomatoes into the crock pot
  4. Add the meat once browned.
  5. Add in seasoning, pepper, onion, and beans
  6. Stir thoroughly
  7. Cook on low for 7 hours, or high for 4 hours
  8. About 30 minutes before the cooking is done, cook the rice
  9. When the rice is done, add it to the chili and stir
  10. Cook for 15 minutes on high

Serve with cheese, sour cream, tortilla chips.

Serves four to six meals.
