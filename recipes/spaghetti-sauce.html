Spaghetti Sauce

Description

This sauce is easy, cooks in a few hours.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti sauce seasoning packet
  • 1 large jar Prego spaghetti sauce
  • 1 14 ounce can diced tomatoes
  • 1 8 ounce can tomato paste
  • green pepper
  • onion
  • celery
  • carrots

Steps

  1. Dice the vegetables
  2. Put the sauce into the crock pot
  3. Add the vegetables
  4. Add in seasoning
  5. Add in tomaotes and paste
  6. Stir thoroughly
  7. Cook on low for 7 hours, or high for 4 hours

Serves six to eight meals.
